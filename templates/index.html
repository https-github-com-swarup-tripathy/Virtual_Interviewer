Background


The current pandemic has forced the whole world to the New Era of DIGITISATION from classroom to the industrial organisations. Everyone was forced to Work from Home for their safety and to follow the pandemic guidelines.

For students like us it gave us an opportunity to build something cool for our industrial organisations who were facing problems majorly in hiring sectors.The only medium of interaction between the interviewers and the candidate has been through the several video conferencing platforms where still it's difficult for interviewers to examine the facial expressions or the body language to assess the candidate. Here the candidate has most of the advantage i.e while video conferencing the webcam of candidates may or may not be good.

So we tech_mercenaries a team who is a participant in Infosys Summer of Ideas came up with an idea described below.



General


We introduce you to Virtual Interviewer which allows the candidate to answer typical interview questions through video. We have used a pre-trained complex Deep Learning algorithm that analyzes the results of the video and provides a summary of what our algorithm percieved to the Talent Acquisition Manager, and how the candidate performed compared to previous candidates.

In the current scenario where the companies are struggling to hire candidates here our Model will make their task much simpler and more accurate in selection of the best candidate for their role.
