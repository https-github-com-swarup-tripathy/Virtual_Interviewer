Future Goals


So currently the model takes in 45 seconds of the video and then takes in the input of each frames and then with the help of the dataset it then classifies the emotions.

Our future aim is that the Recruitment Managers can further be able to upload their own recorded videos and get the output from our model. We also thought of analysing the audio input from the candidate as well in order to get much more detailed analysis about the candidate.

We can further also go with text analysis too where the candidate is asked to write on the question asked by the interviewer and then with the help of NLTK packages and web mining we could see how the candidate is using the words and how the model would be able to determine the usage of most common words.
